My Projects.

During the second and third year of engineering I had been involved in various academic and extra-curricular projects. Lets have a look at some..

College-Fest mobile App

Colleges have a hard time registering students for various events and students are often unaware of the exact details. We come up with a solution and make Website as well as an Android app protoytpe that can register students and issue notifications, reminders and announce winners. The idea can be scaled at various levels to cater needs of competitions, marathons, seminars and any other event invloving mass communications.

Hacking using Kali

A mini-project topic we selected for Networks and security.In a nut-shell we wanted to exploit network security. For this we used Kali Linux and some of its inbuilt functionalitites like ettercap, urlsnarf, Social engineering toolkit, spoofing, cloning etc to grab data being sent from the browser to the server. Using the right procedure and environment we parsed sensitive information like username, password among others. This worked with major websites like Flipkart, Snapdeal, facebook etc.

Omni-directional Vehicle

During the second year, we made a prototype for an RC camera-equipped spy vehicle that can travel in any direction (360 °) unlike traditional vehicles. The camera gave output wirelessly to a laptop as the car was driven places to places. We presented the idea and the prototype in the college fest and also at Avishkar-2014, Mumbai. This concept can be used for numerous applications like survelliance, spying, cleaning machines at malls, wheel-chairs, etc. Interestingly the car had just one wheel!
